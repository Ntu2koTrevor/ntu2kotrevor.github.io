About

Ntuthuko Trevor Dlamini aka TrevorOctober.

  • Early - Late 90’s

    Umlazi (NUZ)

    Born January 16, 1992 in Umlazi Township, Kwa-Zulu Natal. I was raised by a strong single mom. I had a tough upbringing, it motivated me to dream big. From Umlazi Township, I saw what ukushizila is and how many young people never make it out of the township, but I learnt how to survive. In the late 90’s I fell in love with MUSIC, a time when Rnb music and Kwaito music were making hits (in South Africa).

  • 2009 - 2013

    Matric - College

    I completed my matric in 2009 and I had to choose a career field, and surprisingly I chose to study Systems Engineering and Information Technology.

  • Early 2014

    Gap Year

    After completing my diploma, I decided to take a gap year collecting music records and expending my knowledge in Java programming, SQL, and PHP.

  • December 2014 - Present

    Cape Town

    In December 2014 I moved to Cape Town to pursue my career in Software Development.In February 2015 I joined Project Codex, which is a career training institution for software development and agile methodology. I trained for three months. During my training at Project CodeX, I had the opportunity to hone my skills in NodeJs, Git, Github, Travis, Mocha, QUnit, Gulp and Agile Methodology. Since May 2015, I've worked for 3 different companies as a Python Software Engineer.
